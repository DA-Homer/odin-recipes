Burger Recipe

A Mouth-Watering 30-minute Burger

Bite into this juicy 30-minute burger that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 900g ground beef
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar, for each burger
  • 1-2 large onions, cut into slices
  • 1 large tomato, cut into medium-thick slices
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 60g unsalted butter
  • mayonnaise

Method

  1. Add beef to a bowl and mix in the eggs until fully incorporated.
  2. Divide the beef into four equal balls and press them into patties.
  3. Slice the onions and tomatoes.
  4. Turn your grill, or frying pan, on high.
  5. Drizzle some olive oil over the patties and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  6. Place the patties on your hot pan and cook for four minutes before turning.
  7. Season the onions put them in the pan.
  8. Season the bun and toast them in the pan.
  9. Remove the toasted buns and add some mayonnaise to both buns. Add a piece of lettuce, top with tomatoes, and season.
  10. Just before your patties are ready, baste both sides with butter, add the sliced cheese, and cover to melt.
  11. When the cheese has melted, place the patty on the bun, top with onions and serve.
